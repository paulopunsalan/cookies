Peanut Butter Cookies

Rich, nutty, and melt-in-your-mouth delicious, Peanut Butter Cookies are a timeless favorite that never fail to satisfy. With their signature crisscross pattern and golden edges, these cookies strike the perfect balance between sweet and salty, soft and crumbly. Each bite delivers that unmistakable roasted peanut flavor that cookie lovers can't resist.


Originally popularized in the 1930s, Peanut Butter Cookies have remained a beloved staple in home kitchens ever since. Whether you enjoy them plain, dipped in chocolate, or sandwiched with a creamy filling, they're the perfect treat for peanut butter fans everywhere ‚Äî simple to make, comforting to eat, and endlessly nostalgic.



Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup sugar, plus more for sprinkling
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract (optional)
  • ¬º teaspoon coarse salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper; set aside. In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat together peanut butter, egg, sugar, baking soda, vanilla, if using, and salt until well combined.
  2. Using a 1 3/4-inch scoop, scoop dough onto prepared baking sheet, spacing 2 inches apart.
  3. Using the tines of a fork, gently press down on each scoop in a crosshatch pattern.
  4. Sprinkle with sugar and transfer baking sheet to oven. Bake until slightly puffed, 11 to 12 minutes. Cookies will still be soft. Let cool for 5 minutes.
  5. Carefully transfer cookies to a wire rack and let cool completely.
