Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

Rich, fudgy, and dusted with a snowy layer of powdered sugar, Chocolate Crinkle Cookies are as beautiful as they are delicious. Their signature crackled tops reveal a soft, brownie-like center that melts in your mouth with every bite. These cookies strike the perfect balance between deep chocolate flavor and light sweetness, making them a standout treat for any occasion.


Often enjoyed during the holidays but irresistible all year round, Chocolate Crinkle Cookies add a touch of elegance and indulgence to any dessert table. Whether you're baking them for a festive gathering or simply craving something chocolatey and comforting, these cookies are sure to satisfy every sweet tooth.

Directions

  1. Melt chocolate in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water, stirring. Set aside, and let cool.
  2. Sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl; set aside.
  3. Mix butter and brown sugar on medium speed until pale and fluffy, 2 to 3 minutes. Mix in eggs and vanilla, and then the melted chocolate. Reduce speed to low; mix in flour mixture in 2 batches, alternating with the milk.
  4. Divide dough into 4 equal pieces. Wrap each in plastic; refrigerate until firm, about 2 hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  6. Divide each piece into 16 (1-inch) balls. Roll in granulated sugar to coat, then in confectioners' sugar to coat. Space 2 inches apart on baking sheets.
  7. Bake until surfaces crack, about 14 minutes. Let cool on sheets on wire racks.
