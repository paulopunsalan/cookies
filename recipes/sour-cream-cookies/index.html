Sour Cream Cookies

Soft, tender, and delicately sweet, Sour Cream Cookies are a delightful treat that brings a touch of old-fashioned charm to any occasion. The addition of sour cream gives these cookies their signature moist, cake-like texture and a subtle tang that balances their sweetness beautifully. Light and comforting, they're the kind of cookie that feels like a warm hug from the kitchen.


Often topped with a simple glaze or a sprinkle of sugar, Sour Cream Cookies are perfect for tea time, holidays, or everyday indulgence. Whether you enjoy them plain or dressed up with a hint of lemon zest or vanilla, these cookies capture the simple joy of homemade baking ‚Äî soft, comforting, and utterly satisfying.
