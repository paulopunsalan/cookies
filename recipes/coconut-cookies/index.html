Coconut Cookies

Light, crisp, and delightfully fragrant, Coconut Cookies bring a taste of the tropics to your kitchen. Each bite offers a perfect balance of buttery sweetness and the irresistible chew of toasted coconut flakes. Whether enjoyed with an afternoon cup of tea or packed as a sweet snack on the go, these cookies are simple yet full of flavor.


Loved for their golden edges and subtle tropical aroma, Coconut Cookies are a wonderful choice for those who enjoy a dessert that's both comforting and refreshingly unique. Whether you make them plain or add a hint of vanilla or white chocolate, they're sure to become a sunny favorite in any cookie jar.

  3. Add flour; pulse until a crumbly dough begins to form, scraping sides of bowl as needed (do not overmix).
  4. Forming 5 or 6 at a time, scoop level tablespoons of dough, and place in reserved coconut. Roll into balls, coating with coconut.
  5. Arrange balls, about 2 inches apart, on baking sheets.
  6. Bake until lightly golden, 23 to 25 minutes. Cool on baking sheets 1 to 2 minutes; transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
